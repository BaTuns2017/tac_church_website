Our Mission

St. John Anlican Church | Iwo-Ate Missionary Archdeacory shall be Bible-based, Spiritually Dynamic, United Disciplined, Self- supporting, Committed to pragmatic Evanglism, Social Walfare and the Church that Epitomizes the Genuine love of Christ.

Our Mission

To present the Gospel in an irresistible and aceptable manner to the yet to-be reached who are resident in our domain, realizing that we are live in multi-cultural, multi-religious environment!
To Undertake holistic Evangelism of Iwo-Ate land in particula and the nation in general in compliance with the great commission of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Our Cherished

Upholding dignity of the hman person *Humility * Accountability *Transparency *Honesty *Idustry *and Hard Work *Commitment *Openness
Team Spirit *Upholding the Biblical Orthodoxy.
